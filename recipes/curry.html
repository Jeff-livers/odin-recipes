Tofu Curry Recipe

This recipe is gonna go over how to make the wonderful and very tasty tofu curry. Curry is something you can make any time with just a few simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Coconut Milk
  • Ginger
  • Garlin
  • Onions
  • peppers
  • Curry Paste
  • Lime
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat pan and put vegetable oil into it
  2. cook onions and peppers first until soft. after a few minutes add garlic and ginger and cook for a couple more minutes
  3. put curry paste in and stir around until all veggies are coated with the curry paste. once veggies are coaoted add the can of cocnut milk
  4. let simmer for about 15 mins on low
  5. finish up by adding salt, pepper, sugar, and lime
  6. eat and enjoy
